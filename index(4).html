Info!

How Long Do Commissions Take?


                Usually commissions will take around a week or two. If it is more complicated or of higher skill, it may take three.

                I do have other work to attend to, but I'll notify the customer directly if I need to take any longer. Life happens and 
                all that.

What are shipping prices like?


                Depending on location, prices can fluctuate. 
                
                I operate out of Chesterfield, VA, so especially if overseas, prices will go up. I would recommend
                checking out a shipping price generator from my location to your own.
                
                Each keychain is about .5 lbs and pins, ranging on size, can go up to
                around .95 lbs.

Where do you ship to?


                I ship almost anywhere! As long as the price is right!
                
                If shipping prices are a little high, such as for Singapore, I am willing to negotiate
                a fair price even if it means lowering some of my own (not actually this just to fill up space). 

                Obviously if it is a non-physical commission, there is no cost in shipping.
